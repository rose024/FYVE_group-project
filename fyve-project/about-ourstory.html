ABOUT US

Welcome to FYVE Festival, a community-based festival in Vancouver, BC. We are passionate about raising awareness and funds to support those affected by the opioid crisis and the stigma surrounding addiction.

Vancouver has been hit hard by the opioid crisis, with devastating effects on individuals, families, and communities. We believe that everyone deserves support and compassion, regardless of their circumstances. FYVE Festival is our way of giving back to the community and making a positive impact on those who need it the most. Our festival aims to create a safe and inclusive space where people can come together, enjoy live music, art, and culture, and learn about the challenges and solutions related to addiction and recovery. We believe that education and awareness are key to reducing the harm caused by the opioid crisis, and we strive to provide opportunities for people to engage with this important issue.

Through our festival, we hope to bring attention to the complex social, economic, and health issues related to addiction and homelessness. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to recover, and we are committed to working with our community partners to provide support and resources to those who need it the most.
We are driven by a sense of compassion and empathy for those who are struggling with addiction and homelessness. We believe that together, we can make a difference and create an equal society for all. No matter who you are, or where you’ve come from; you matter to us. Join us on our mission to support those in need and make a positive impact on our community.

OUR MISSION & VALUE

Our mission at FYVE Festival is to raise awareness and funds to support those affected by the opioid crisis and homelessness in Vancouver, BC. We believe that everyone deserves access to resources and support to overcome addiction. Through our festival, we strive to create a safe and inclusive space for people to learn about addiction and recovery while promoting a culture of compassion and empathy. We the founders of FYVE Fest value advocating for the vulnerable, and being the voice of change. The importance of having a community and being there for one another is the beauty of human nature; the power of unity and love can heal any wound. We want to show the world that we as people can unite and support our community and the many other communities struggling with the burdening grasp of addiction.

THE CRISIS

Vancouver is known for its natural beauty and fast paced lifestyle. With record breaking housing prices and the outrageous cost of living the individuals living here are struggling beyond measure.

Many of the streets in the lower mainland are riddled with poverty and addiction due mainly to the opioid crisis. The opioid crisis began when fentanyl; a horrific drug, was introduced into the streets; not only in Vancouver but worldwide. Fentanyl is a drug that is almost 50 times stronger than heroin; it has taken the lives of millions and has destroyed countless families and loved ones alike. Addiction can affect anyone. It doesn’t pick and choose between people. Simply put; it can happen to you.
