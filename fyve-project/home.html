Why FYVE?

At FYVE festival we wanted to create a safe space for people of all walks of life to come together to prevent drug addiction and homelessness in the lower mainland of British Columbia Canada. As of recent we have seen a Spike in both homelessness and drug addiction/overdoses within our community. The five festival wants to spread awareness and encourage people to educate themselves as to what is going on in our backyard.

Our goal for this event is to facilitate resources and engaging educational activities for the youth in our community to learn about the risks of partaking in illicit activities.

The FYVE festival family hopes that all attendees leave with good memories and new Perspective on topics such as drug addiction and homelessness.

At the festival, your favourite Canadian artist such as Drake, JoJo Mason and Maddison Gifford will be playing your favourite songs all weekend long. We will also be collaborating with the D.A.R.E program (add link) and special guest speakers to host information sessions where they will provide resources and engaging activities to spread the word about Drug Addiction prevention & Homeless Awareness.
