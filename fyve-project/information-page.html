FYVE Schedule

Artists Information

Drake

A diamond recording music artist who has been making hit singles since the 2000s and one of the world's most famous music artists, he is also a fellow Canadian who is deeply interested in the cause. FYVE FEST introduces Drake!

Pink Pantheress

An up and coming artist From London, England, who is taking over the music world by storm. She’s made hit songs with top charting artists like: Lil Uzi Vert, Baby Keem, and GoldLink.

Kid Cudi

Kid Cudi is one of the biggest names in Hip Hop, who’s songs are favourites of multiple generations of fans. He has made some of hip hop's most recognizable songs like ‘Pursuit of Happiness’ and ‘Day ‘n’ Night’ which have inspired millions of people. Cudi has also always been one of the music industry's most vocal proponents for mental health and drug awareness.

Kaytranada

Kaytranada is an up and coming DJ who has entranced the entire music industry. This Haitian born artist grew up in Montreal. He has always been vocal about the importance of mental health, we are grateful to have him at FYVE FEST!

Boslen

Boslen is Vancouver’s most well known up and coming artist. He’s a fan favourite amongst Vancouver locals and has produced songs under Capitol Records.

Subdidi

Subdidi is one of Vancouver’s biggest local DJs. As someone who lives in the city he cares very deeply about this cause.

Jojo Mason

A rising Candian country singer, born and raised in the heart of Vancouver, BC where he developed a love for country music at a young age. He is best known for his songs “Broken Umbrella” & “Let me Down Easy”. Any one who is around Mason is guaranteed laugher and a good time.

Madisyn Gifford

A Candian singer song writer who has a passion for making heartbreaking lyrics, something to dance to. The 23 year old was born into a musical family, so songwriting is in her blood. Her latest album “I have ur guts” will have you on the dance floor until the place shuts down.

Adrienne Robles Music

A canadian singer/song writer duo from Vancouver, BC. The duo varies from RnB to pop music. There debut album “Adrienne” has listeners crying, dancing and screaming. The Duo has been playing music together since 2014 and have no plans to stop anytime soon!

Gwyn Love

A Oklahoma City girl who embraces all things pop. Singed with 604 Records in 2020, Her songs have been sibling the charts ever since. Her spunky and caramastic vibe resonates with all listeners, through her upbeat tracks and heartfelt lyrics.
